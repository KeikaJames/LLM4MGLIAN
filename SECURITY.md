## 1. 项目宗旨 / Project Purpose

本项目以保护、传承和弘扬中华民族优秀传统文化为首要宗旨。

本项目所涉蒙古语文本、民族语言文字材料、历史文献、地方知识及相关文化资料，系中华民族优秀传统文化博大精深的重要组成部分。传统蒙古语资料的数字化整理、文本标注、模型辅助学习和工具链开放，对于保护民族语言文字资源、促进民族团结、推动学术繁荣和实现文化传承具有重要意义。

This project is established with the primary purpose of protecting, preserving, and promoting the outstanding traditional culture of the Chinese nation.

The Mongolian language materials, ethnic language resources, historical texts, local knowledge, and related cultural materials involved in this project form an important part of the profound and extensive outstanding traditional culture of the Chinese nation. The digital preservation, text annotation, model-assisted learning, and open tooling of traditional Mongolian materials are important for preserving ethnic language resources, promoting ethnic unity, advancing academic prosperity, and supporting cultural continuity.

## 2. 仓库性质 / Repository Scope

本仓库用于发布、维护和协作改进本项目相关的：

- 模型权重；
- Tokenizer；
- 模型配置文件；
- 推理配置；
- 校验信息；
- 模型卡；
- 必要说明文件；

This repository is used to publish, maintain, and collaboratively improve:

- model weights;
- tokenizers;
- model configuration files;
- inference configuration files;
- checksums;
- model cards;
- necessary documentation;
- auxiliary files related to model loading, validation, evaluation, and usage.

This repository is not intended to serve as a raw corpus repository, scan archive, full transcription repository, peer-review material repository, or third-party works distribution repository.

## 4. 原始语料边界 / Raw Corpus Boundary

本仓库的开源性、开放协作性和公共文化服务属性，主要适用于模型权重、Tokenizer、模型配置、工具链、说明文档及不可还原原文的加工成果。

本仓库原则上不发布以下内容：

- 完整原始语料库；
- 完整转写文本；
- 扫描件、影印件、图片原件；
- 连续长段原文；
- 未公开同行评议材料；
- 个人信息、敏感信息或保密信息；
- 未经授权的第三方受保护作品表达；
- 足以替代原始作品正常阅读、购买或授权使用的内容集合。

如任何贡献、提交、Release、Issue、Pull Request 或说明文件中误包含上述内容，维护者可以采取隐藏、移除、撤回、替换、重写历史记录或限制访问等措施。

The open-source, collaborative, and public-cultural-service nature of this repository primarily applies to model weights, tokenizers, model configurations, tooling, documentation, and processed outputs that cannot reconstruct original texts.

This repository generally does not publish:

- complete raw corpora;
- full transcriptions;
- scans, facsimiles, or original images;
- long continuous passages of source text;
- unpublished peer-review materials;
- personal information, sensitive information, or confidential information;
- unauthorized third-party protected expressions;
- content collections that may substitute for normal reading, purchase, or licensed use of original works.

If any contribution, commit, release, issue, pull request, or document mistakenly includes such content, maintainers may hide, remove, withdraw, replace, rewrite history, or restrict access to the relevant materials.

## 5. 版权与模型边界声明 / Copyright and Model Boundary Statement

我们尊重作者、译者、整理者、出版机构、馆藏机构、数据库权利人、资料提供方及其他潜在第三方权利人依法享有的合法权益。

同时，我们认为，著作权保护的是依法构成作品的具体表达。著作权不应被扩张解释为对语言文字本身、思想、观点、事实、方法、语言规律、统计结果、索引结构、标注体系、模型参数、向量表示、质量评估指标或不可还原原文的研究结论享有当然控制权。

本仓库反对将著作权异化为阻断民族语言文字资料保护、传统蒙古语资料传承、学术研究、模型工具发展和中华民族优秀传统文化数字化保护的工具。

We respect the lawful rights and interests of authors, translators, compilers, publishers, archival institutions, database right holders, material providers, and other potential third-party right holders.

At the same time, we take the position that copyright protects specific protectable expressions. Copyright should not be expansively interpreted as granting automatic control over language itself, ideas, opinions, facts, methods, linguistic patterns, statistical results, index structures, annotation systems, model parameters, vector representations, quality-evaluation metrics, or research conclusions that cannot reconstruct original texts.

This repository opposes the use of copyright as a tool to obstruct the preservation of ethnic-language materials, the transmission of traditional Mongolian resources, academic research, model-tool development, or the digital protection of the outstanding traditional culture of the Chinese nation.

## 7. 请报告的问题 / Issues to Report

请通过安全渠道报告以下问题。

Please report the following issues through the security contact channel.

### 7.1 模型与权重安全 / Model and Weight Security

- 模型权重被篡改；
- 权重文件与声明校验值不一致；
- 权重文件含异常 payload、恶意代码或可疑二进制内容；
- 模型加载过程触发非预期网络访问、文件写入或命令执行；
- 模型文件格式存在可利用的反序列化风险；
- 模型输出可稳定复现敏感信息泄露。

- model weights have been tampered with;
- weight files do not match declared checksums;
- weight files contain abnormal payloads, malicious code, or suspicious binary content;
- model loading triggers unexpected network access, file writes, or command execution;
- model formats create exploitable deserialization risks;
- model outputs can reliably reproduce sensitive information leakage.

### 7.2 Tokenizer 安全 / Tokenizer Security

- Tokenizer 文件被篡改；
- Tokenizer 规则导致异常崩溃、拒绝服务或资源耗尽；
- Tokenizer 包含异常特殊 token、隐藏控制字符或误导性 token 映射；
- Tokenizer 行为与版本声明明显不一致；
- Tokenizer 可能诱导下游系统绕过安全过滤、日志审计或访问控制。

- tokenizer files have been tampered with;
- tokenizer rules cause abnormal crashes, denial of service, or resource exhaustion;
- tokenizer files contain abnormal special tokens, hidden control characters, or misleading token mappings;
- tokenizer behavior materially differs from version declarations;
- tokenizer behavior may induce downstream systems to bypass safety filters, logging, or access controls.

### 7.3 数据泄露与版权风险 / Data Leakage and Copyright Risks

- 仓库中误包含原始语料、完整转写文本、扫描件或未授权长文本；
- 仓库中误包含个人信息、敏感信息或同行评议材料；
- 模型输出可稳定还原训练材料中的长段原文；
- 示例、测试文件或说明文档中包含未经授权的第三方受保护表达；
- 发布包中误包含受限制数据、内部文件或平台导出文件。

- the repository mistakenly includes raw corpus materials, full transcriptions, scans, or unauthorized long text passages;
- the repository mistakenly includes personal information, sensitive information, or peer-review materials;
- model outputs can reliably reconstruct long passages from training materials;
- examples, test files, or documentation contain unauthorized third-party protected expressions;
- release packages mistakenly include restricted data, internal files, or platform exports.

### 7.4 供应链与分发安全 / Supply Chain and Distribution Security

- Release 文件与校验信息不一致；
- 镜像、下载链接或外部依赖被污染；
- 第三方托管文件与本仓库声明版本不一致；
- 安装、加载、转换或推理流程中存在可利用缺陷。

- release files do not match checksum information;
- mirrors, download links, or external dependencies are compromised;
- third-party hosted files differ from the version declared in this repository;
- installation, loading, conversion, or inference workflows contain exploitable flaws.

## 8. 非安全问题 / Non-Security Issues

以下问题通常不作为安全漏洞处理，但可通过普通 Issue 或 Discussion 反馈：

- 模型能力不足；
- 生成质量、翻译质量或转写质量问题；
- 一般性幻觉、错误回答或风格问题；
- 项目路线、许可证或发布策略的一般讨论；
- 不包含可复现安全风险的泛泛批评；
- 与本仓库无关的第三方平台问题。

The following are usually not treated as security vulnerabilities and may be reported through regular issues or discussions:

- insufficient model capability;
- generation, translation, or transcription quality issues;
- general hallucinations, incorrect answers, or style issues;
- general discussions about project direction, licensing, or release strategy;
- broad criticism without reproducible security impact;
- third-party platform issues unrelated to this repository.

如问题涉及数据泄露、版权风险、个人信息、模型滥用或可复现安全风险，请按安全问题报告。

If the issue involves data leakage, copyright risk, personal information, model misuse, or reproducible security impact, please report it as a security issue.

## 10. 处理流程 / Response Process

维护者收到报告后，将尽力按照以下流程处理：

1. 确认收到报告；
2. 初步判断问题是否属于安全范围；
3. 复现并评估影响；
4. 必要时临时隐藏、撤回、替换或限制相关文件；
5. 制定修复方案；
6. 发布修复版本或安全公告；
7. 在适当范围内感谢报告者贡献。

After receiving a report, maintainers will make reasonable efforts to:

1. acknowledge receipt;
2. determine whether the issue falls within the security scope;
3. reproduce and assess impact;
4. temporarily hide, withdraw, replace, or restrict relevant files where necessary;
5. prepare a remediation plan;
6. publish a fixed version or security advisory;
7. acknowledge the reporter’s contribution where appropriate.

对于涉及数据泄露、版权争议、个人信息或同行评议材料的问题，维护者可优先采取临时下架、限制访问、替换样例、移除文件、重写历史记录或暂停发布等措施。前述临时措施不当然表示项目方承认相关权利主张成立。

For issues involving data leakage, copyright disputes, personal information, or peer-review materials, maintainers may prioritize temporary removal, access restriction, example replacement, file deletion, history rewriting, or release suspension. Such temporary measures do not necessarily constitute an admission of the validity of any rights claim.

## 11. 负责任披露 / Responsible Disclosure

为保护用户、贡献者、研究者和项目本身，请报告者遵守负责任披露原则：

- 在修复前不要公开漏洞细节；
- 不要利用漏洞访问、下载、扩散或重构受限制内容；
- 不要尝试从模型中批量提取训练材料、个人信息或第三方受保护表达；
- 不要上传、传播或公开敏感样例；
- 不要对项目基础设施、托管平台或用户系统进行破坏性测试；
- 不要将安全报告用于勒索、胁迫或公开施压。

To protect users, contributors, researchers, and the project itself, reporters should follow responsible disclosure principles:

- do not publicly disclose vulnerability details before remediation;
- do not use vulnerabilities to access, download, distribute, or reconstruct restricted content;
- do not attempt to extract training materials, personal information, or third-party protected expressions from the model at scale;
- do not upload, distribute, or publicly disclose sensitive examples;
- do not conduct destructive testing against project infrastructure, hosting platforms, or user systems;
- do not use security reports for extortion, coercion, or public pressure.

我们尊重善意报告者，并将在合理范围内记录其贡献。

We respect good-faith reporters and will acknowledge their contributions where appropriate.

向本仓库提交贡献时，请遵守以下要求：
